Metasploitable 2

∵ Halfwhit ∴ 2023-01-26 ∞ 5'

Metasploitable 2 is an intentionally vulnerable virtual machine running linux to perform penetration testing and security research. Over the course of several posts I will be exploring this machine and writing up my findings. In this first post I will be assessing what ports and services are open in order to plan our next move. Due to the box being a locally hosted VM, enumerating via DNS will be skipped.

With that out of the way, it's time to scan! I will be using the tool nmap to conduct my scan:
